About

Shanghai Restaurant brings you delicious Chinese and Asian inspired food, hosts provocative events, and displays compelling local artwork presented with a retro-modern flair making The Shang one of Ottawa’s hottest social destinations!

BOOK CHINA-CHINA DOLL as the hostess for your next event.

CATERING is available for larger orders upon special request. Please Call us for details.

EVENT BOOKINGS in our dining room are available for private parties and other special occasions. Contact us directly by phone at 613-233-4001 or email us with the details to discuss your booking.

Are you a visual artist, band, or performer interested in showing off your talent? Call us for more information!
